Beyond the Classroom

How can Conect help me or my child?

Here's what we do

CONNECT can provides students with a multitude of features in order to help them in the classrom and beyond. Not only does the service aim to help students excel academically, we want to foster sucess in all aspects of life.

Our Enhanced Schedule, Planner, and Goal-setting Functions set reminders, keep a detailed calendar, and prioritizes your tasks based on urgency and relevancy. It gives the option to set goals, both long-term and short-term. Writing out one's goals and the steps required to achieve them is extremely effective for people to refer to and utilize.

In addition, games that work to improve focus, memory, and engage in mindfulness therapy are an important aspect of CONNECT. Perhaps something just as important is the connectivty. CONNECT allows parents and teachers to be apart of a student's development and provide support for them as well. Teachers and Parents can set goals, give feedback and give points for achieving goals.
