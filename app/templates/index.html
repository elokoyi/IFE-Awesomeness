CONNECT is the first ever prouductivity and mindfulness service specifically tailored towards the needs of students with ADHD and ADD. We believe in a generation of students that have unlimited possibilities of success when provided with the right tools.

Connect gives students the tools they need to build their future.

  • On average, every classroom of 30 students has 1 to 3 children with ADHD.
  • 40% of youth with diagnosable (but not necessarily diagnosed) ADHD symptoms don’t get treatment
  • 35% of teens with ADHD eventually drop out of school.
  • 65% of children with ADHD have problems with defiance, non-compliance and other problems with authority figures, including verbal hostility and angry outbursts.

These numbers don't show the whole story.

Assistive Technology

Tools that help a person plan, organize, and keep track of their calendar, schedule, task list, contact information, and miscellaneous notes. These tools manage, store, and retrieve such information. Also included is a medication assistant that give reminders and updates about your medication and dosages.

Skill development games

CONNECT provides a game library of 50+. games that help students practice and improve things like reading, writing, focus, behavior, and comprehension. We work together with other game companies in order to get well-developed and fun games that help students learn.

Connectivity

Like the name suggests, CONNECT offers a platform that allows all the support systems in a students life to be connected to their journey. The app offers a system in which a student’s parents and teachers can have access to their progress and set goals for them.
